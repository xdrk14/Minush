About Me

Hello! I'm Minush Pathinisekara, a 15-year-old student from Ja-Ela, Sri Lanka.

Having recently completed my O Levels at Lyceum International School Wattala, I'm eager to continue learning and exploring new challenges.

Beyond academics, I dedicate time to practicing Katana fighting. This martial art is more than just a hobby; it's a discipline that cultivates focus, respect, and a deep appreciation for tradition and precision.

My goal is to balance my academic pursuits with personal growth, always striving for discipline and understanding in everything I do. This website shares insights into my interests and the topics I've researched.
